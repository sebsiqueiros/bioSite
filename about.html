Biography

Armando grew up in a neighborhood where gangs were common. Growing up without a father made it difficult to find the right direction, and he eventually became involved with a gang at a young age. At the time, he was more focused on proving how tough he was than thinking about the consequences of his choices.

The biggest turning point in his life came when he became a father. Having a child made him realize he wanted a different future for his family. He knew he did not want his child to grow up without a father, so he decided to leave that lifestyle behind and completely separate himself from the people who were holding him back.

Today, Armando is proud to be a husband and father who puts family first. He enjoys spending time with his children, fishing, hiking, and playing video games. His story is proof that people can change, no matter what mistakes they made in the past, if they are willing to work hard and commit to building a better future.
