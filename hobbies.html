Hiking

One of Armando's favorite hobbies is hiking. He enjoys being outdoors and challenging himself while taking in the scenery. Hiking has become an important part of his life because it helps him stay healthy and reminds him how far he has come.

Fishing

Armando learned to fish from his grandfather when he was young. Today, fishing allows him to relax and spend quality time with his family while creating new memories with his own children.

Family Time

More than anything else, Armando enjoys spending time with his wife and children. They are his biggest motivation and the reason he continues working hard every day. Family has become the center of his life and the foundation for the future he always wanted.
